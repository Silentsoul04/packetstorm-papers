Dear friends !
We are happy to announce that CFP for Ukrainian cybersecurity conference UISGCON14 is open!
The conference will take place in Kyiv, Ukraine, 26th of October 2018. Venue will be Ukrainskiy Dim in a downtown.

You have opportunity to submit for presentation,workshop,village or other activity you want to make on the event!
Following topics are hot this year: Internet privacy, network security, cryptography, governance, innovative attack/defense strategies, forensics/incident response, IoT security, hardware hacking, mobile and wireless security, security automation, threat Intelligence, cloud security.
All vendor or commercial talks will be banned by program committee.
We also have lightning talks section for newcomers, so you can pitch your brief ideas too.

You can submit your activity on EasyChair by the link: https://easychair.org/cfp/UISGCON14
Our conference page can be found here: https://uisgcon.org
Or you can follow us on twitter: https://twitter.com/UISGCON
