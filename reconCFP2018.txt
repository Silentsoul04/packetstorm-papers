                                                                       a
You may send additional supporting parser exploits in other formats.   a
                                                                       a
Please include:                                                        a
                                                                       a
 - Speaker name(s) and/or handle                                       a
 - Contact information, e-mail, and cell phone (optional)              a
 - Presentation title                                                  a
 - Presentation subtitle                                               a
 - Length of presentation (30 or 60 minutes)                           a
 - Description of the presentation                                     a
 - Brief biography                                                     a
 - If available, presentation supporting materials (website, code,     a
   paper, slides, outline, ...)                                        a
 - Why it is cool / why you want to present it                         a
 - If you have any objections to recording the presentation            a
 - Country of origin (Passport origin, current geographical location)  a

   the procedure early)                                                a

   assistance from us.                                                 a
