-> Preamble:

Dear audience,

the 3rd row of hack4 the two days security conference in berlin
asks yours kindly to send in papers and workshops for the crowd.

-> Where is the con?
As every year hack4 is in Berlin.

-> When is the con?
29th and 30th of december

-> Who should attend?
As you are reading fd, you.

-> What is different at hack4?
We try to select a good mixture between talks and workshops. Most topics
are hands-on or have direct practical applications.

-> What are possible topics?
- Reverse Engineering Hardware/Software/Protocols
- Exploits and vulnerabilities
- Shellcoding on different architectures
- How (not) to code
- How to break things
- Bypass protections in security related hard/software
- Cryptography attacks
- General hacking topics
- Forensics
- probably much more not listed here

-> What you can expect?
A cosy event with cool people and hack'ish atmosphere.
